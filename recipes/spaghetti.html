Spaghetti

Delicious spaghetti, just like mom used to make.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Premade Tomato Sauce
  • Spaghetti Noodles

Steps:

  1. First, brown the ground beef in a pan over medium heat.
  2. While your beef is cooking, fill a pot with water and set it over high heat, bringing it to a boil.
  3. Then, once your beef is fully cooked, strain out any excess grease and add tomato sauce to the pan, bringing it to a simmer over a low heat.
  4. Once your water is boiling, add your noodles and set a timer for 8 minutes.
  5. When the timer is up, strain your noodles, turn off the burner for the meat and sauce and you are ready to have some delicious spaghetti!
  6. Enjoy!
